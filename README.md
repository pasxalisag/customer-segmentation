## Business Objective
The objective of this project is to segment pharmacy customers based on their purchasing behavior, in order to identify high-value accounts, growth opportunities, and at-risk customers.

---

## Project Overview
This project applies customer segmentation techniques to B2B pharmaceutical sales data, focusing on business-relevant metrics such as revenue contribution, purchase frequency, and volume.

The goal is to translate customer clusters into clear, actionable segments that can support sales prioritization and account management strategies.

---

## Key Questions
- Which pharmacy accounts contribute most to total revenue?
- Are there distinct customer segments with different purchasing behaviors?
- Which customers should be prioritized, developed, or reactivated?

---

## Tools & Approach
- Business-oriented customer metrics
- Segmentation logic (clustering techniques)
- Clear interpretation of segments
- Actionable recommendations per segment
